HMP Podcast Survey Resuts

Survey Design

Objective

The objective of this survey was to get community feedback in order to inform the content direction of the Healthy Me PA podcast. Survey participants were invited to express their interest on a variety of proposed topics ranging from health policy to reviews on health and fitness trends.

Distribution

We created two different versions of this survey to distribute to our existing Mailchimp email list and more broadly to Facebook and Twitter via advertising.

Total Completes = 155

Survey Results

Podcast Listeners

Q1: Do you listen to podcasts?

Those who answered “No” to question 1 were asked a follow up question, “Would you listen to a podcast produced by Healthy Me PA?”. All 65 respondents answered “Yes”.

Show Descriptions

Next, we tested five different types of podcast shows. Below are descriptions of each.

Show Description
Medical Miracles & Unsolved Medical Mysteries We see them in the news and online, but how much do we really know about the incredible breakthroughs and unsolved mysteries that have happened in the medical community? Our hosts break down one per episode.
Health Policy for Dummies Let’s face it: we’re not all experts in health policy. We take an in-depth look at health policy hot topics and demystify them for the average Joe.
Grocery Store Hacks How to make smart choices for your body and your wallet. File it under the topics they should teach in school!
Mom Hacks Local moms discuss health tips for kids, how to manage everyday challenges and tips for doing it all and keeping it healthy!
Reviews You Can Use Kombucha, meditation, activity tracking apps - we try the latest health and fitness trends and give you our honest take on if it’s worth the hype.

We asked respondents to rate how likely they were to tune into each show on a scale of “Not at all likely” to “Extremely likely”.

Below is a summary of all those that were either “Very likely” or “Extremely likely” to listen to each show.

Comparatively, the shows that responds are ‘Very’ or ‘Extremely’ to tune into are:

  1. Grocery Hacks
  2. Reviews
  3. Medical Mysteries
  4. Health Policy
  5. Mom Hacks

Respondents were then asked directly which show they would rather tune into out of the five presented:

In order of likelihood, the shows that responds would want to tune into are:

  1. Reviews
  2. Medical Mysteries
  3. Grocery Hacks
  4. Health Policy
  5. Mom Hacks

As a follow up, respondents were also asked about their second choice of show that they would tune into.

As for a second choice of show, respondents preferred the following:

  1. Reviews
  2. Health Policy
  3. Grocery Hacks
  4. Medical Mysteries
  5. Mom Hacks
  6. None of These
